You can go from zero to entered in the SaveToWin jackpot in about five minutes. All you need is a Solana wallet and some USDC. This guide walks you through each step.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the app">
    Open the SaveToWin web app in your browser. You'll land on the home page which shows the current jackpot size, epoch countdown, and a summary of how the vault works.

    <Tip>
      Bookmark the app URL so you can check your streak and jackpot status before each draw.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Navigate to the **Vault** page (`/vault`) and click **Connect Wallet**. SaveToWin supports:

    - **Phantom** — the most widely used Solana wallet
    - **Solflare** — a strong alternative with hardware wallet support

    Select your wallet from the connection modal. If you don't have a Solana wallet yet, download Phantom from [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) or Solflare from [solflare.com](https://solflare.com), create a new wallet, and save your seed phrase somewhere safe before returning here.

    <Warning>
      Never share your seed phrase with anyone, including SaveToWin. The app only ever asks you to sign transactions — it never requests your private key.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get USDC on Solana">
    SaveToWin accepts **Solana USDC** (mint: `EPjFWdd5AufqSSqeM2qN1xzybapC8G4wEGGkZwyTDt1v`). If you don't have any, you have a few options:

    - **Buy directly on a CEX** (Coinbase, Kraken, etc.) and withdraw USDC to your Solana wallet address.
    - **Bridge from another chain** using a cross-chain bridge if you already hold USDC on Ethereum or another network.
    - **Swap on-chain** using Jupiter ([jup.ag](https://jup.ag)) to convert SOL or another Solana token to USDC.

    <Note>
      You also need a small amount of SOL in your wallet to pay Solana transaction fees. A few cents worth of SOL is enough for many deposits and withdrawals.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deposit into the vault">
    On the Vault page (`/vault`), enter the amount of USDC you want to deposit and click **Deposit**. Your wallet will ask you to approve the transaction.

    Once confirmed on-chain:

    - Your USDC moves into the SaveToWin vault.
    - Your TWAB tracking begins immediately for the current epoch.
    - Your streak starts at 1× — it increments automatically when you remain deposited into the next epoch.

    <Note>
      Your streak starts automatically the moment your deposit is confirmed. You don't need to do anything else — just stay deposited to keep it growing.
    </Note>

    You can deposit any amount. Larger balances increase your TWAB weight, which improves your odds proportionally.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your status">
    After depositing, the Vault page shows your personal status panel:

    - **Your balance** — USDC currently in the vault
    - **Your TWAB** — your time-weighted average balance for the current epoch
    - **Streak** — your current streak level and multiplier (1× through 32×)
    - **$SAVE points** — your lifetime accumulated points

    You can also visit the **Leaderboard** (`/leaderboard`) to see where you rank among all depositors by lifetime points, and the **Draws** page (`/draws`) to review past draw results.

    <Tip>
      Check back before each draw to see how large the jackpot has grown. If previous epochs had no winner, it could be significantly larger than the base amount.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawing

You can withdraw your USDC at any time from the Vault page. There is no lock-up period. Your principal is always accessible.

<Note>
  Withdrawing your full balance resets your streak to zero. If you want to preserve your streak multiplier, keep at least some USDC in the vault.
</Note>
