You can withdraw your USDC from SaveToWin at any time. Withdrawals are processed in a queue: you submit a request, and a keeper bot automatically pulls liquidity from the underlying lending strategy and delivers it to your wallet — no second signature needed. Most withdrawals settle within about five minutes.

## Two things to know before you withdraw

**Partial withdrawals preserve your streak.** As long as your vault balance stays above zero after the withdrawal, your streak count and multiplier carry forward unchanged into the next epoch.

**A full withdrawal to zero resets your streak.** If you withdraw everything — leaving nothing in the vault — your streak counter resets to zero. Your next deposit starts a new streak at 1×.

<Warning>
  Withdrawing your entire balance resets your streak to zero. Your multiplier will restart at **1×** on your next deposit. If you have a high streak (and therefore elevated winning odds), consider leaving a small amount in the vault to preserve it.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Resetting your streak does not affect prizes you've already won. Any USDC you received from a previous draw stays in your wallet. Only future winning odds are affected.
</Note>

## How to withdraw

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the vault">
    Open the SaveToWin web app and navigate to the **Vault** page (`/vault`). Connect your wallet if you haven't already.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Withdraw section">
    Below the deposit form you'll see the **Withdraw** panel. Your current vault balance is shown — use the **Max** button to pre-fill the maximum withdrawable amount, or type a specific amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    Type how much USDC you want to withdraw, or click **Max** to withdraw as much as possible. If you click Max, the form will flag that this will reset your streak so you know before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Queue the withdrawal">
    Click **Queue withdrawal**. Your wallet opens and asks you to approve the transaction. Confirm it. The vault burns your stwUSDC tickets and creates a withdrawal receipt on-chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your USDC">
    A keeper bot monitors pending withdrawals and settles them automatically — usually within five minutes. Your USDC arrives in your wallet without any further action from you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the withdrawal queue

SaveToWin uses a two-phase withdrawal system backed by the Kamino liquid lending sleeve:

**Instant settlement (most withdrawals):** When liquidity is available in the liquid portion of the vault, the keeper settles your withdrawal almost immediately. In practice, the five-minute window covers the time the bot needs to pull funds from the lending strategy.

**Queued settlement (larger amounts):** For very large withdrawals that require the keeper to redeem from deeper liquidity, the process is the same from your side — you submit one request and wait — but settlement may take slightly longer while the strategy unwinds.

Either way, you submit a single transaction and your USDC arrives automatically. You do not need to return to the app or sign anything else.

<Info>
  Only one withdrawal request can be in flight at a time per vault. If the vault is currently processing another user's withdrawal, the form will show **Vault busy — try again shortly**. This usually resolves within a few minutes.
</Info>

## Protecting your streak

If you want to exit most of your position while keeping your streak alive, withdraw everything except a small amount. Even $0.01 left in the vault is enough to preserve your streak counter into the next epoch.

| Action | Streak outcome |
|---|---|
| Partial withdrawal (balance > 0) | Streak preserved |
| Full withdrawal (balance = 0) | Streak resets to 0 |
| No withdrawal this epoch | Streak increments |
