## How SaveToWin works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit USDC">
    Connect a Solana wallet and deposit any amount of USDC. You receive tickets proportional to your deposit — these represent your position and determine your prize odds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn yield automatically">
    Your USDC earns a blended 10.3% APY target from two uncorrelated sources: 70% into fixed-yield real-world reinsurance (PT-ONyc) and 30% into Kamino USDC lending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your streak">
    Stay deposited across consecutive epochs and your winning odds multiply — 1×, 2×, 4×, 8×, 16×, up to 32×. The streak grows automatically as long as you hold a position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Win prizes">
    Each epoch, a Switchboard VRF draw selects a winner weighted by TWAB-adjusted odds. Prizes roll over if no valid winner is found, growing by 10% each time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your principal is always protected. You can withdraw your full deposit at any time — winning is a bonus, not a risk.
</Note>
